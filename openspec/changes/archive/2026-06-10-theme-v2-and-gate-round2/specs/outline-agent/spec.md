## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Content-page budgets and chapter figure coverage
Detail-level page budgets SHALL count content pages only (structure pages excluded), and every
results/validation/discussion chapter SHALL include at least one figure- or chart-bearing page.

#### Scenario: Structure pages do not consume the budget
- **WHEN** a normal-detail deck is planned
- **THEN** the prompt budgets 8-11 content pages with structure pages explicitly excluded
